## FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

**1. Is this actually free?**
Yes. The audit is 100% free to use. You don't even need to create an account or provide an email address to see your results. We only ask for your email if you want to save the report or talk to us about getting discounted AI credits.

**2. How do you calculate the savings?**
We maintain a constantly updated database of official pricing from OpenAI, Anthropic, GitHub, Cursor, and others. Our deterministic engine looks for common inefficiencies: paying for retail when team plans are cheaper, duplicate tools that serve the same purpose (like Cursor + Copilot), and unused capacity.

**3. Will you try to sell me something?**
If your audit shows that you could save more than $500 a month, we will offer you a free consultation with Credex. Credex provides discounted AI infrastructure credits to startups. If you're fully optimized or your savings are small, we'll just say "Great job" and leave you alone.

**4. What happens to my data?**
We don't require any PII (Personally Identifiable Information) to run the audit. The tool configurations you enter are stored locally in your browser until you choose to share or save the report. If you enter your email, it is stored securely and never sold to third parties.

**5. Which AI tools do you support?**
Currently, we audit spend for ChatGPT, Claude, GitHub Copilot, Cursor, Google Gemini, Windsurf, and direct API usage for OpenAI and Anthropic. We add new tools to our pricing database as they become relevant to startup engineering teams.
